    The Culprit

    At some point in our lives we have all experienced that one neighbor that doesn’t seem to care for the comfort of others. Either playing obnoxiously loud music or partaking in other activities in rather uncomfortable volume. Seeing as one on one confrontation isn’t ideal for most people. Here are some ways you can ask for them to lower their volume without problems.

      Resolutions
  • You can file a report with your local police precinct and they will respond when there isn’t an emergency to handle.
  • You can also contact your tenant association, building owner, or super for help if a neighbor in your building is often noisy.
  • Now if the problem is an air conditioner, ventilation equipment, or exhaust device. You will be contacted by the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) within 10 days of the complaint being filed and a inspection will be set up.
